WINWIN Agreement Canvas

Use each tile to align expectations and shared value.

W — Well‑defined Outcomes

Define success in observable, testable terms.

  • Primary objective(s) and scope boundaries
  • Deliverables (e.g., report, dashboard, SOP)
  • Completion criteria & acceptance tests

I — Information Architecture

What data, content, and structure do we need?

  • Inputs (forms, datasets, templates)
  • Storage & versioning approach
  • Outputs (views, exports, briefs)

N — Negotiated Constraints

Make constraints explicit to reduce risk.

  • Timeframe & milestones
  • Capacity, tools, compliance
  • Assumptions & dependencies

W — Workflow Clarity

Agree on how work flows end‑to‑end.

  • Roles & responsibilities (RACI)
  • Handoffs & review gates
  • Change control process

I — Iterative Improvements

Plan feedback loops and adaptation.

  • Cadence (weekly/bi-weekly)
  • Feedback channels & DoD
  • Retrospectives & learnings

N — Next Steps & Ownership

Close with concrete actions and owners.

  • Immediate actions & dates
  • Named owners & backups
  • Communication plan

Your Win / My Win

Surface shared value explicitly to prevent misalignment.

Your Win

  • Clarity on deliverables and timelines
  • Measurable impact & traceability
  • Usability for real-world decisions

My Win

  • Maintainable, readable CSS/HTML
  • Semantic structure & accessibility
  • Reusable components & patterns

Scopes & Measures

Define scope, indicators, and evidence upfront.

Scope

Boundaries of what is in/out for this cycle.

  • In-scope features & artifacts
  • Out-of-scope items
  • Risks & mitigations

Indicators

How we’ll know we’re succeeding.

  • Process: timeliness, quality checks
  • Output: deliverable completeness
  • Outcome: usage & decision influence

Evidence

Data sources and means of verification.

  • Documents, datasets, tickets
  • Reviews, sign‑offs, audits
  • Surveys, analytics, logs
