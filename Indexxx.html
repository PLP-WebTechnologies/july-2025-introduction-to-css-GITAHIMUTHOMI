Our Mission

From 2020 to 2025, Win Win Strategies is dedicated to building sustainable and resilient solutions to human vulnerabilities—environmental, social, political, and economic—to enhance community resilience. This strategic period is centered on combating hunger, in line with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger.

Focus Areas (2020–2025)

  • Resilient food systems & crop diversification
  • Partnerships & innovation for community food access
  • Technology & infrastructure for food distribution
  • Nutrition-sensitive programming & education

Diversification & Resilience

Support diverse, climate-resilient crops and local production to ensure food supply even in shocks.

Innovative Partnerships

Co-create solutions like urban gardens, mobile markets, and school nutrition programs with communities.

Infrastructure & Tech

Enhance storage, transport, and data systems to connect producers with those in need efficiently.

Nutrition & Education

Advance awareness and training to tackle malnutrition and promote healthy, balanced diets.
